Hidden Places Worth Exploring

  • Begnas Lake (Pokhara) — Less crowded alternative to Phewa Lake
  • Tansen (Palpa) — A hill town with amazing views and history
  • Rara Lake — Largest lake in Nepal, offbeat and stunning
  • Barpak (Gorkha) — Rebuilt after earthquake, great for homestays
  • Chisapani Trek — 2-day trek with forest & mountain views
